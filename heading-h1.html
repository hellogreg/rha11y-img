Getting the bookmarklet

To get the rha11y-heading-h1 bookmarklet, drag the following link to your bookmarks bar. You may also click the link directly to use it on this page for testing. (Refresh the page to reset it.)

rha11y-heading-h1

The rha11y-heading-h1 bookmarklet helps you verify that your page has only a single <h1> tag. When run, the script opens a modal that lists (and counts) all the page's <h1> elements.
